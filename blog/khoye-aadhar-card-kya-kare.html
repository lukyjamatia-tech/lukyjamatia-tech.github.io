Aadhar Card Kho Gaya? Ghabraiye Mat — Yeh Karein

Har saal lakhs Aadhar cards India mein kho jaate hain. Lekin ab ek dedicated platform hai jahan finder aur owner connect ho sakte hain.

Step 1 — FindBack par Report Karein

Agar aapka Aadhar card kho gaya hai, turant findback.org par lost report submit karein. Free hai.

Step 2 — Police Complaint

Nearest police station mein FIR ya complaint darj karein. FindBack ka FIR drafting tool bhi use kar sakte hain.

Step 3 — UIDAI Portal

uidai.gov.in par jaake duplicate Aadhar download karein ya reprint order karein.

Step 4 — Finder Se Connect Hon

Agar kisi ne aapka Aadhar card findback.org par found report kiya hai, toh aapko notification milega aur aap seedha connect kar sakte hain.
