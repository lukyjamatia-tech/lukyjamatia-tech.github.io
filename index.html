About FindBack S1
India's trusted lost & found platform

🎯 Our Mission

FindBack S1 was built to solve a real problem — thousands of items are lost every day across India, and there was no single trusted place to report and reunite them.

🌍 How It Works

When you lose something, post it on FindBack. When you find something, post it too. Our platform intelligently matches lost and found posts so items can be returned to their owners.
