About FindBack S1
India's trusted lost & found platform

🎯 Our Mission

FindBack S1 was built to solve a real problem — thousands of items are lost every day across India, and there was no single trusted place to report and reunite them.

🌍 How It Works

When you lose something, you post it on FindBack. When you find something, you post it too. Our platform intelligently matches lost and found posts so items can be returned to their rightful owners.

🛡️ Our Values

We believe in transparency, community trust, and privacy. Your data is never sold. Our platform is and will always remain free to use.
