About FindBack S1
India's trusted lost & found platform

🎯 Our Mission

FindBack S1 was built to solve a real problem — thousands of items are lost every day across India, and there was no single trusted place to report and reunite them. We're on a mission to change that, one item at a time.

🌍 How It Works

When you lose something, you post it on FindBack. When you find something, you post it too. Our platform intelligently matches lost and found posts so items can be returned to their rightful owners — quickly and safely.

🛡️ Our Values

We believe in transparency, community trust, and privacy. Your data is never sold. Our platform is and will always remain free to use for anyone who needs to find what they lost.

👤 The Team

FindBack S1 is built and maintained by a passionate solo founder from India, driven by the belief that technology can solve everyday human problems. This is Season 1 — just the beginning.
