हमारा मक़सद — Our MissionOUR MISSION

FindBack क्यों बना और हम क्या करना चाहते हैंWhy FindBack was built and what we stand for

हर चीज़ खोई नहीं होती —
बस रास्ता भूल जाती है घर का.
Nothing is truly lost —
it just forgets its way back home.
"खोई चीज़ ढूंढने के लिए अब सिर्फ एक जगह चाहिए — FindBack."
"One place to find what India lost — FindBack."

India में हर रोज़ हज़ारों लोग अपनी क़ीमती चीज़ें खो देते हैं — mobile, wallet, documents, और सबसे दर्दनाक — अपने अपने। लेकिन एक dedicated platform नहीं था जहाँ खोये हुए और मिलने वाले दोनों एक जगह आ सकें।

Every day in India, thousands of people lose something precious — mobiles, wallets, documents, and most painfully — their own loved ones. Yet there was no single dedicated platform where the lost and the found could meet.

FindBack India का पहला dedicated lost & found platform है — जो technology, community और police को एक साथ जोड़ता है। हम सिर्फ एक app नहीं हैं — हम एक civil movement हैं।

FindBack is India's first dedicated lost & found platform — connecting technology, community and police together. We are not just an app — we are a civil movement.

हमने एक बड़ी problem देखी — India में हर रोज़ हज़ारों चीज़ें खोती हैं और हज़ारों लोग missing होते हैं। लेकिन कोई एक dedicated जगह नहीं थी जहाँ खोने वाला और ढूंढने वाला दोनों एक साथ आ सकें। Police के पास resources कम हैं, existing platforms इसके लिए बने नहीं हैं, और आम इंसान को पता ही नहीं कि करे तो क्या करे। यह gap real है — और हम इसे technology और community की मदद से solve करने की कोशिश कर रहे हैं।

We saw a clear gap — every day in India, thousands of items are lost and thousands of people go missing. Yet there was no single dedicated place where the person who lost and the person who found could connect. Police are stretched thin, existing platforms were never built for this, and ordinary people have no idea what to do. This gap is real — and we are trying to bridge it using technology and community.

Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 18 April 2026 | findback.org

📦 1. What We Collect

We collect: name and email (registration); profile photo and ID (NGO/Police portal); post content, photos, location, contact details; and usage data via Google Analytics 4.

🎯 2. How We Use It

To operate FindBack, authenticate users, display posts, generate FIR drafts, detect fraud, improve the platform, and communicate service updates.

🔒 3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore) with industry-standard encryption. Here is exactly what is stored where:

  • Posts (lost/found/missing) — Firestore collection: posts. Stores item name, type, location, description, contact, photos (base64), timestamp, user UID.
  • User Profiles — Firestore collection: users. Stores name, email, profile photo URL, phone, registration date, provider (email/Google).
  • Messages — Firestore collection: messages. Anonymous messages between users about a post.
  • Reports — Firestore collection: reports. Fraud/abuse reports submitted by users.
  • Police Notifications — Firestore collection: police_notifications. Auto-generated alerts sent to verified police/NGO users when a post is made in their district.
  • NGO/Police Profiles — Firestore collection: ngo_users. Organization name, type, district, state, ID proof (pending admin approval).

Firebase security rules restrict access: users can only read/write their own data. Police/NGO portal data is restricted to verified accounts only. No data is stored on any third-party server other than Google Firebase (Google Cloud, India/Asia region).

🚫 4. We Never Sell Your Data

FindBack does NOT sell your personal data. We share data only with law enforcement when legally required, authorized NGO/Police portal users (district-level only), and service providers (Firebase/Google) who help operate our platform.

✅ 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data and withdraw consent. Contact legal@findback.org to exercise these rights.

👶 6. Children's Privacy

FindBack is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
