Delete Your FindBack Account

This page explains how to request deletion of your FindBack account and associated data. FindBack is operated by FindBack (findback.org).

How to request deletion

Send an email to legal@findback.org from the email address registered with your FindBack account, with the subject line: Delete My Account.

We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days.

What is deleted

Upon account deletion, we permanently delete: your account credentials, profile information, verification selfie and ID images, your lost/found/missing item posts, and uploaded photos.

What may be retained

We may retain limited records where required by law, or where a report is linked to an active fraud investigation, for up to 90 days after deletion, after which they are permanently removed. Anonymised analytics data that cannot identify you is not deleted.

Chat data

Conversations with the Manphikha AI assistant are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers linked to your account.
